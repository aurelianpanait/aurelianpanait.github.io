V2G Fleet Dispatch Optimization for Primary Frequency Containment Reserve (FCR)

Modelled and compared Uniform vs. Smart V2G dispatch strategies for a 150-EV fleet participating in Primary Frequency Containment Reserves (FCR). Vectorized simulation in NumPy/Pandas over real grid frequency data (France 2019–2021, 10s resolution). Smart strategy: efficiency 95% vs. 86% (Uniform), OBC operating time reduced to ~6.7% (bang-bang control). Battery degradation via Thévenin equivalent circuit and throughput-based cost model (€150/kWh, 3000 cycles). Net revenue ~€4/EV/month after depreciation. Supervised by Pierre Dumont, PhD — MINES Paris · PSL (2026).
